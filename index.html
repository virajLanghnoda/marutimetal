ABOUT US

Maruti Metal was established over 12 years ago with a commitment to crafting high-quality kitchenware products. Our extensive collection is thoughtfully designed to maximize convenience, efficiency, and durability in every kitchen. We take pride in our ability to merge innovation with functionality, ensuring that every product we manufacture enhances the cooking experience.

As a trusted OEM supplier, we collaborate with leading companies in the industry, delivering premium stainless steel and other high-grade material products that meet international quality standards. With a diverse catalog of over 50+ products—including lunch boxes, knives, graters, pizza cutters, tea strainers, and more—Maruti Metal has become a reliable name in the kitchenware manufacturing sector.

At Maruti Metal, we take pride in manufacturing and distributing premium kitchenware products that embody both excellence and elegance. Our unwavering commitment to quality ensures that every product we create meets the highest standards of durability and functionality.

Our team of over 70+ skilled workers operates with precision and dedication, utilizing advanced machinery and stringent quality control measures.
